WEATHER APP DESIGN

I designed and programmed a weather application to put into practice my knowledge in front-end languages, especially React.

For this work, I designed 12 pieces to use as background images that graphically describe weather conditions and time. I then developed the app to identify the weather and time zone of the requested location and deliver the information using its corresponding image and icon.
